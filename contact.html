Frequently Asked

What types of projects do you work on?

I specialize in web design, graphic design, and art direction. This includes brand identity, websites, digital experiences, print design, and creative consultation for various industries.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. Simple projects may take 2-4 weeks, while comprehensive branding projects can take 6-12 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes, I work with clients globally. I'm comfortable collaborating remotely and have experience working across different time zones and cultural contexts.

What is your design process?

My process includes four key stages: Discovery & Research, Concept Development, Design Execution, and Implementation. Each project begins with understanding your goals and ends with a solution that exceeds expectations.
